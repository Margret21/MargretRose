About

A little about my life.

  • 1994-In Progress

    Growing Up

    I grew up in a military family which included a lot of moving and traveling. I was born in San Antonio, Texas, moved to Germany for 3 years, then moved to Virgina and finally ended up in Spokane, Washingotn where my parents retired.

  • 2000-Now

    Arts and Crafts

    I have always done really well in school and although art was my favorite subject, I always imagined I'd go into something more technical. I actually enjoy solving math problems and taking tests. (I know weird.) Then when I graduated high school I heard about graphic design and I realized I could actually make a living creating art.

  • 2012-Now

    Eastern Washington University

    EWU's visual communication design program is growing more every year and without my amazing teachers I don't know that I would have stayed with this program. I have worked, lived and learned at Eastern for 5 years now and I wouldn't change a moment.

  • Starting Now

    My Future is so Bright

    I know that graduating was just the beginning. There is so much that I want to accomplish. I want to travel and learn and grow as much as I can. My goal would be to reach as many people as I can with my work and I hope I can find a company that will help me do that.
